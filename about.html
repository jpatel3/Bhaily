About Bhaily Village

Overview

Bhaily (Pincode: 391410) is a village located near the western periphery of Vadodara city in Gujarat, India. Situated along the Vishwamitri river, it has seen significant growth and development in recent years, becoming a popular residential area with numerous modern housing projects.
